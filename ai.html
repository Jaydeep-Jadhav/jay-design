Designing at the speed of AI.

AI doesn't replace my thinking. It amplifies my research, creativity, execution, and decision-making, so I spend more time solving meaningful user problems and less time on everything around them.

Introduction

AI is my design partner, not my replacement.

I don't use AI to skip the design process. I use it to ask better questions, explore more directions, validate assumptions faster, automate the repetitive work, and turn ideas into working products sooner.

Every decision still comes from human judgment, empathy, and product thinking. AI just gives me a better way to think, create, and iterate.

Being a designer today isn't only about making beautiful interfaces. It's about orchestrating the right tools to solve the right problems.

My AI philosophy

Think better. Not just faster.

AI should amplify human intelligence, not replace it. Here's what I actually use it for:

Discover better questions Explore multiple design directions Reduce repetitive work Speed up research synthesis Improve documentation Prototype ideas rapidly Challenge my own assumptions Learn continuously

The final decisions are always mine.

AI principles

The rules I don't break

  • 1Human first. Technology should enhance human experiences, not replace human understanding.
  • 2Judgment over automation. AI generates possibilities. I make decisions.
  • 3Curiosity over convenience. I use AI to think deeper, not to think less.
  • 4Systems over shortcuts. Good workflows create better products.
  • 5Learning never stops. AI evolves every week. So do I.
