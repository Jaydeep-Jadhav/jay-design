About

Designer from Pune. Obsessed with why people do what they do.

I started in visual design and grew into the full product loop: research, strategy, interface, and iteration. I care about products that respect people's time and attention.

What actually pulls me into this work is the problem, not the pixels. Give me something broken or confusing and I want to know why before I touch a screen. And when something I've built genuinely changes how someone gets through their day, that's the part of the job I'd do for free.
